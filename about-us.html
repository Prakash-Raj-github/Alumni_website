About Us

One dream one focus

TPGITAA was established to help students to know more about the outside environment after graduation by conducting yearly alumni meets. Experienced alumnus are strongly encouraged to nurture the students in all aspects especially in preparation for competitive exams , they also help students to get placed in many reputed organizations.
