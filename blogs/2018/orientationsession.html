TPGIT First Year Orientation Session

   19 Sept 2018

Objectives


  • To motivate students to take up carrier in Engineering
  • To understand Opportunities in field of Engineering

Overview


  • What Do Engineers Do.
  • Effect of Globalization.
  • India as Manufacturing Hub for world.
  • Role of engineers in Knowledge Economy.
  • Who should become Engineer.
  • Branches in Engineering and it's applicability.
  • Why become An Engineer

They discussed about Career Options for ECE like Core Jobs,Non-core Jobs,Government Jobs,Open Your Own Start up,Higher Education

Core Jobs



Companies for core field

Wipro Lighting, Broadcom, Texas Instrument, Schnider Electric, Bajaj,Electricals,Freescale Semiconductors, Alstom Corporate, Bharat Heavy Electricals, Havells,Crompton Greaves, Neolex Cables, HBM Power Systems, Exide Industries, Tata,Electric, CESC and so on.

Network Communication related companies : Siemens, BSNL, Sony Ericsson, Reliance and Nokia.

VLSI and Embedded System Jobs

IC(Integrated Circuits) is compact version of Circuits.Digital IC that perform Arithmetic and Logical operation is called processor.The Chip which contain processor and peripherals those help to processor called controller.VLSI : Design of Processor or Controller.Embedded System : Choose different processor or controller that already designed to application and program it as application requirement.It contain two different process make IC. They are Fabrication and Design.Using silicon, Fabricate the IC in FAB is called Fabrication.Ready the Plan to Fabricate chip is called Design.Plan should be satisfied with every constraint that relate to logic, Interference,Charge storage, ESD, Power Consumption, Performance, Efficiency and so on.



Non-Core Jobs



Positions : Assistant Software Engineer, Junior Software Engineer, Programmer,.Net Engineer, Quality Assurance Manager, Developer, and Business Analyst.

Companies : Wipro, TCS, Accenture, Infosys, HCL and so on.

Skills needed : C/C++ languages and excellent communication skills.
