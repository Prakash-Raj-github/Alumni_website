Electronics and Communication Engineering Association’s Technical Symposium Inauguration By Mr. Anandaraj B.E (2000 ECE)

   21 NOV 2019

Mr. Anandaraj from 2000 ECE was invited as the Chief Guest for National level technical symposium in ECE Department and he provided a Career Guidance speech during inaugural ceremony. He discussed and interacted with students regarding the various kinds of software used in the industry and their importance. He instructed students regarding facing interview in both attitude and technical aspects. He had an interactive session with students on VLSI domain. Also he promised students that they can reach him at any time for software related queries and for placement related activities.
